Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Justice for Dr. Silvio Grixti ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you visit and use the justiceforgrixti.com website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy by design. Our petition platform is designed to collect minimal personal data. We do not log IP addresses, unique identifiers, or detailed metadata about your visit.

  • Personal Data: When you sign the petition, you may provide your name, locality, and optionally your email address. If you opt-in for our newsletter, your email address is collected for that purpose. We do not verify this information.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect cookies or track user behavior beyond basic, anonymized page view counts (simulated for demonstration purposes).

2. Use of Your Personal Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To add your name and locality to the petition in support of Dr. Silvio Grixti.
  • If you opt-in, to send you newsletters and updates regarding Dr. Grixti's case via Proton Mail.
  • To respond to your inquiries if you contact us directly via email.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third parties.

3. Data Security (Proton Mail)

We utilize Proton Mail for all email communications and, in a real-world scenario, for secure data management. Proton Mail provides end-to-end encryption, ensuring that your communications with us and any data we manage related to the petition remain confidential and secure. We do not store sensitive user data directly on our website servers.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to any personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request that we correct any inaccurate personal data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data from our records.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at justiceforgrixti@proton.me.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: justiceforgrixti@proton.me
