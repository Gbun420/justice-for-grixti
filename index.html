About Dr. Silvio Grixti

Medical Career

For decades, Dr. Silvio Grixti has served as a dedicated family doctor in Malta, known for his commitment to patient care and community service.

Community Involvement

Beyond his medical practice, Dr. Grixti has been actively involved in community initiatives and previously served as a Member of Parliament.

Current Situation

Dr. Grixti is currently facing legal challenges related to disability benefit applications. This platform supports his reinstatement and reputation restoration.

Understanding the Case

The legal proceedings involve allegations related to disability benefit applications. It's important to note that:

  • Dr. Grixti maintains his innocence
  • Many former patients attest to his ethical medical practice
  • The case involves complex legal interpretations of medical documentation
  • Several individuals involved in similar cases have been acquitted

This petition seeks to highlight Dr. Grixti's decades of faithful service and request fair consideration of his contributions to the community.
