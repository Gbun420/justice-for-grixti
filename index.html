About Dr. Silvio Grixti

Medical Career

For decades, Dr. Silvio Grixti has served as a dedicated family doctor in Malta, known for his commitment to patient care and community service. His practice has touched thousands of lives across multiple generations.

Community Involvement

Beyond his medical practice, Dr. Grixti has been actively involved in community initiatives and previously served as a Member of Parliament, representing the people with integrity and dedication.

Current Situation

Dr. Grixti is currently facing legal challenges related to disability benefit applications. This platform supports his reinstatement and the restoration of his hard-earned reputation.

Understanding the Case

The legal proceedings involve complex allegations related to disability benefit applications. It's important to note that:

  • Dr. Grixti maintains his innocence and is cooperating fully with authorities
  • Many former patients and colleagues attest to his ethical medical practice over decades
  • The case involves intricate legal interpretations of medical documentation
  • Several individuals involved in similar cases have been acquitted based on specific circumstances

This petition seeks to highlight Dr. Grixti's decades of faithful service and request fair consideration of his contributions to the Maltese community.
